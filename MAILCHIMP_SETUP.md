## 🚀 STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

### STEP 1: Get Your Mailchimp API Key (2 minutes)

1. **Login to Mailchimp**: https://mailchimp.com/
2. Click your **profile icon** (top right)
3. Click **"Profile"** → **"Extras"** → **"API keys"**
4. Click **"Create A Key"** button
5. **COPY the API key** (it looks like: `abc123def456ghi789...`)
   - Keep this window open or paste it somewhere safe!

---

### STEP 2: Get Your Mailchimp Audience ID (1 minute)

1. Still in Mailchimp, click **"Audience"** in the left menu
2. Click **"All contacts"**
3. Click **"Settings"** dropdown → **"Audience name and defaults"**
4. Look for **"Audience ID"** (looks like: `a1b2c3d4e5`)
5. **COPY the Audience ID**
   - Keep this handy!

---

### STEP 3: Connect in Formspree (2 minutes)

1. **Go to Formspree**: https://formspree.io/forms
2. **Login** to your Formspree account
3. Find your form **"myykdyrv"** and click on it
4. Click the **"Plugins"** or **"Integrations"** tab
5. Find **"Mailchimp"** in the list
6. Click **"Add"** or **"Connect"**
7. **Enter your info:**
   - **API Key**: (paste from Step 1)
   - **Audience ID**: (paste from Step 2)
8. **Optional - Map Fields:**
   - `EMAIL` → email (automatic)
   - `FNAME` → name
   - `PHONE` → phone
9. Click **"Save"** or **"Enable"**

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Problem: "Invalid API Key"**
- Make sure you copied the entire key
- Try generating a new API key in Mailchimp

**Problem: "Audience not found"**
- Double-check your Audience ID
- Make sure it's the right format (letters + numbers)

**Problem: Contacts not appearing**
- Check Formspree form settings to ensure integration is enabled
- Verify the audience is active in Mailchimp
- Check Mailchimp's "unsubscribed" list

Caring For Your Permanent Jewelry

✦

Your Sacred HaLo piece is designed to stay with you — a permanent bond that moves through life alongside you.

Daily Wear

Live freely with your piece. Shower, sleep, and move through your day without worry.

What to Avoid

  • Chlorine pools & hot tubs — Weakens the bond and can damage the chain
  • Harsh chemicals — Cleaning products, bleach, and strong detergents
  • Excessive pulling — Treat your piece with gentle care
  • Saltwater — Extended exposure can cause tarnishing over time

If Removal is Needed

Use small scissors or nail clippers to carefully cut the jump ring (the small welded section). Your piece can be re-welded at your next appointment.

Lifetime Free Re-Welds

Free re-welds for life apply to chains purchased from Sacred HaLo by the original purchaser.

Important: Free re-welds are non-transferable and only available for the original wearer. Chains purchased elsewhere or transferred to another person will incur a service fee.
